Memorial Day Parade

Neshanic Volunteer Fire Company

🏁 Monday, May 25 — Step Off at 9:30 a.m.

Information For Participants

All local scout troops, youth organizations, civic groups, and others are welcome to participate. Please arrive between 8:45 and 9:15 a.m.

Groups walking in the parade should assemble on the firehouse lawn (Maple Avenue side). FD, EMS, and other vehicles should line up on Maple Avenue and around the curve on Blackpoint Road.

There is no preset order of march — units will line up in order of arrival.

  Throwing candy or items to spectators is prohibited — children darting into the street creates a serious safety hazard.

  Please respect the solemnity and dignity of Memorial Day. No sirens unless leaving the parade route to respond to an emergency.

Information for Spectators

The parade steps off from the firehouse at 9:30 a.m.

Parade Route: Maple Avenue → Elm Street → Pearl Street → Olive Street → Pleasant Run Road.

Immediately following the parade there will be a brief ceremony in front of the firehouse.

Parking

Parking is available in the NVFC Meadow by the Elm Street Bridge and on various side streets in the village.

There is no parking on the streets that make up the parade route.

Wreaths & Flowers

Organizations wishing to lay wreaths or flowers during the ceremony should check in at the registration table prior to the parade.

Wreaths delivered in advance may be placed in the designated staging area in front of the firehouse.
